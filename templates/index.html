Resume Customization

Welcome to the Resume Customization Tool! This tool utilizes advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to analyze and compare your resume with a job description.

Feature 1 identifies key skills and action verbs in your resume and the job description, providing a detailed comparison to highlight alignment and potential improvement areas.

Feature 2 uses the power of LLMs to rephrase specific resume points, enhancing their impact by emphasizing relevant skills and action verbs. This feature is designed to refine your resume for maximum effectiveness.

Analyze Resume and Job Description

Copy and paste your resume and the job description into the fields below to see how they match.

Rewrite Resume Point

Enter a specific point from your resume, along with a skill and an action verb, to get a suggested rewrite.
